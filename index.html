2016 University Ranking Comparison

by Rachel Thorp and Carina Sauter

This visualization is based on a Kaggle dataset based on the Times Higher Education World University Rankings. The data shown here is the ranking for 2016. Out of the entire ranking, we chose to compare scores in 5 categories - Teaching, Income, Citations, Research and International Students.

This visualization was created with code from Alvaro Graves, Nadieh Bremer and Paul Glenn and Shir Nehama.
